Pente — Simplified Chinese (zh-Hans) Translation Review
========================================================

Hi! Thanks for looking this over. Pente is a two-player board game played on
a 19x19 grid. It's similar to Gomoku (五子棋) but with one big twist: you can
CAPTURE opponent stones by sandwiching a pair of them between two of yours.
The English name "Pente" comes from the Greek word for "five".

How to win:
  1. Get five of your stones in a row (五子连珠), OR
  2. Capture five pairs of opponent stones (夹吃五对 = 10 stones total)

Please check each string below for:
  - Naturalness (does it sound like a native speaker wrote it?)
  - Game-term accuracy (especially "capture" — we're using 夹吃)
  - Any awkwardness or ambiguity
  - Suggest a better phrasing if one comes to mind

Write your comments in the [NOTES] line under each entry. Thanks!
